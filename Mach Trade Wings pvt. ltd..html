ABOUT US

Mach Trade Wings pvt. ltd., established on 24th July 1995, has emerged as a trusted name in the logistics and trade services industry. It has been a long journey wherein we have honed our skills in freight forwarding & custom clearance. Our mission is to simplify global trade and provide end-to-end solutions that cater to the diverse needs of businesses involved in international commerce. Our extensive range of services is designed to ensure that your goods move seamlessly across borders, whether by air or sea. Our core offerings include comprehensive import and export forwarding services, covering both air and sea freight. We specialize in door-to-door delivery, taking care of every step in the logistics chain—from the point of origin to the final destination. This includes meticulous coordination and handling, ensuring that your shipments are delivered on time, every time, with the highest levels of efficiency and care. Mach Trade Wings Pvt Ltd is Govt. licensed Custom broker in addition to being a freight forwarder, thus providing comprehensive and fully inhouse import and export services.
