ABOUT US

Jaquaar Industrial Equipments is leading air compressor manufacturer & distributor from 2000 in the sales and service of compressed air equipment. We are one of only a select few companies that both sell and service most major brands of compressed air equipment on the market today. Since Jaquaar Industrial Equipments is not limited to one brand, make or model of compressor equipment like other air compressor distributors; we can specify the best compressed air, vacuum pump or dryer solution for our customer's application. Jaquaar Industrial Equipments Industries is a leading provider of high-quality air compression solutions tailored for various industrial applications. Founded on a commitment to reliability and innovation, the company has grown significantly, establishing a strong reputation in sectors such as manufacturing, construction, and automotive.
